UKSEDS National Rocketry Competition Rocketry – 2019/2020

Our society is taking part in UKSEDS National Rocketry Competition this year. We are challenged with designing, building and launching a mid-power rocket with the primary goal of reaching the greatest apogee possible.

Building our rocket and making it fly will take a team of dedicated team of students to master the art of flying rockets.

Key Dates For 2019/2020 Competition

Registration Deadline - ​Monday 11th November 2019
Design & Build Report Due - ​Monday 20th April 2020*
Teams Must Launch By - ​Monday 27th July 2020*
Launch report Due - ​Monday 10th August 2020*
*Dates shown here are different to the ones on the official websites, due to a change in plan (dates are pushed back a month)
Unfortunately, due to COVID-19, our team was unable to build and launch our rocket for the competition. Our society will instead try our best to learn from our mistakes and improve our rocketry ideas and skills for next year’s competition. Ad Astra!
