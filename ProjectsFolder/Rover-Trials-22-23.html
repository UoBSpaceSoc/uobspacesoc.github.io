Rover Trials

Rover Trials challenges student teams to design, construct and operate a rover for an analogue sample return mission to Mars. Using our past experience our society will conquer the "Mars environemnt". This is an exciting competition for us, as our society consists of many students from different degrees trying to tackle this challenge and any problems that will lie ahead. Join us for this years competition where we will be holding different workshops and activities.
