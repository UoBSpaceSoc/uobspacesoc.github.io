A little bit about University of Birmingham Space Society!

ABOUT US

We are one of the University of Birmingham’s newer societies, aimed to motivate, engage and enable members to get involved in the UK, European and global space communities. With us, you will challenge yourself to develop innovative ideas and engage with industry experts; through outreach you will inspire the next generation to pursue STEM in higher education. Through competing in rocket design, Martian rovers, space law and medicine, or attending our events with experts in the space sector, you will take your first steps towards your future careers and take a giant leap as leaders in the space community.
